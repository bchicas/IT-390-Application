About Our Website

This website is a project created by IT 390 students. The website is for people who want to look for books to read that may be searching for something new to read. The website shows books of various genres that people can look over and continue to pursue if the book peeks their interests.

Website Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people who enjoy reading books and are looking for more to read. The website show books recommended based on preferences. People are able to access this website to look for books to read.

Sites like Goodreads have 125 million members, 3.5 billion books, and 430 million monthly page views; this site is on the smaller side of things, connecting new readers who haven't picked up a book in a long time to getback into the habit. We connect you with a selection of good first books to dive into as a reader starting your journey.
